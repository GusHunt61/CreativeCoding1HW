Types of Horror Game There are many different type of horror game, here are some examples
  1. Analog horror
    Analog horror focuses on a feeling of fear which came from an older technology, it often uses VHS recordings as a method of conveying this.
  2. Psycological horror
    Psycological horror is a type of horror which doesn't nessicarily have many jumpscares, or much gore, but rather sustains a feeling of suspense throught the game.
  3. Mob horror
    Mob horror is often zombies, it is where a great number of enemies are posing a threat to a much smaller number of people.
  4. Action horror
    5. Action horror is the opposite of psycological horror, it means that it is active, and rather that focusing on a sense of suspense it focuses on a sense of action and the fear that that brings.
